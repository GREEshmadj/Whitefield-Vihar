About Us

Whitefield Vihar

The resort offers the perfect getaway experience, far removed from the hustle and bustle of city life. Surrounded by lush greenery, it provides a refreshing escape into nature where tranquility meets comfort.
Since 2014, we have upheld a proud tradition of excellence, creating unforgettable stays for every guest. Whether you're seeking relaxation or adventure, we offer a wide range of fun-filled activities and engaging events designed to create cherished memories.
From sunrise strolls through scenic landscapes to vibrant evening gatherings under the stars, every moment here is crafted to be captured and remembered. No matter the occasion, we’ve got you covered with everything you need to make it truly special.
